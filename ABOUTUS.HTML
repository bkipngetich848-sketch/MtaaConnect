About Us

Who We Are

Fairdeal Furniture is Kenya premier home and office furniture provider, offering a wide range of high-quality, stylish, and affordable pieces. Our passionate and professional team is dedicated to delivering an exceptional customer experience, helping you create spaces that reflect your vision and lifestyle.

Our Journey

From a humble store in Mombasa in 2007, Fairdeal Furniture has grown into a trusted household name across Kenya. Today, we proudly operate 10 showrooms nationwide, including 4 in Nairobi, and branches in Mombasa, Malindi, Nakuru, Eldoret, and Kisumu.

To meet the growing demand for customized furniture, we established a dedicated manufacturing unit focused on creating personalized designs. Whether its a unique home setup or a tailored office solution, we bring your ideas to life.
