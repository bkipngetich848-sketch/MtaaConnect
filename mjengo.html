Mjengo


Mjengo Limited (Malbros): Established in 1991, this company transitioned from a building materials wholesaler to one of East Africas largest manufacturers of agro-commodity products, including rice, snacks, and rice cakes.

Mjengo Flexi Limited: Founded in 2012 by David Kiragu, this company provides end-to-end residential construction, architectural, and interior design services.
